About TrueGrain Refinishing

TrueGrain Refinishing is a family-owned business dedicated to bringing new life to your kitchen cabinets. With over 20 years of combined experience, our team specializes in high-quality cabinet refinishing and refacing. We use premium materials and a meticulous process to ensure a durable, factory-quality finish.

Why choose us? Our focus is quality. We never cut corners, even when it’s more expensive. Some of our competitors are cheaper, but we take the time to make sure you’re 100% happy. We stand by our work with a satisfaction guarantee.

At TrueGrain, we believe your kitchen should inspire joy. Let us transform your cabinets and make your dream kitchen a reality.
