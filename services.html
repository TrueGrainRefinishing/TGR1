Our Services

Cabinet Refinishing

We expertly paint or stain your existing cabinets for a fresh new look, using durable finishes that showcase the wood's natural grain or a smooth modern color.

Cabinet Refacing

Love your layout but want a new style? We replace your cabinet doors and drawer fronts and refinish the framework to give you a whole new kitchen aesthetic without a full remodel.

Hardware & Accessories

We install new hinges, handles, and knobs to complement your refinished cabinets. We can also add accessories like soft-close hinges, pull-out shelves, and more to upgrade functionality.
